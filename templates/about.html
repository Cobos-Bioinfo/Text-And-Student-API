About {{name}}

This project started as an exercise inspired by Day 29 of the 30 Days of Python challenge, but quickly became something more, my first look at what full-stack development may look like.

I chose FastAPI over Flask to work with a modern, fast framework that’s widely used in today’s Python ecosystem. It was my first time building a web backend, and I wanted to learn something powerful and future-focused.

This was also my first time writing HTML and CSS. I didn’t know much about styling, forms, or layout but I learned by doing. Every button, color, and responsive fix was a new skill picked up along the way.

Building this app helped me practice Python, understand how frontends and backends work together, and gain confidence in solving problems.


It’s not perfect, but it’s mine.
